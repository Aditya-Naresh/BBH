1. What is network marketing and its advantages?

Network marketing, also known as multi-level marketing (MLM), is a business model where individuals become independent distributors for a company's products or services. They earn commissions not only on their sales but also on the sales made by those they recruit to join the network. Advantages include:

  • Flexibility and autonomy
  • Unlimited earning potential
  • Personal development and skill-building
  • Low startup costs
  • Opportunity to build a residual income stream
