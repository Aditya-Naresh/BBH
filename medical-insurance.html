Medical Insurance

1. What is medical insurance and its importance?

Medical insurance, also known as health insurance, covers the cost of medical and surgical expenses incurred by the insured. It ensures access to quality healthcare without financial strain. Medical insurance helps you:

  • Avoid financial ruin due to medical bills
  • Access timely and quality medical care
  • Reduce stress and focus on recovery

2. Why take out medical insurance?

Take out medical insurance to:

  • Protect yourself and your family from financial losses due to medical emergencies
  • Benefit from cashless hospitalization and hassle-free claims
  • Enjoy tax benefits and savings
  • Stay financially secure and independent

3. When to take out medical insurance?

Consider taking out medical insurance:

  • When you're young and healthy to lock in lower premiums
  • When you start working or switch jobs
  • When you get married or start a family
  • When you have a history of medical conditions or chronic illnesses
  • When you're approaching retirement or have aging parents to support
