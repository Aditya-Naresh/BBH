Life Insurance

1. What is life insurance and its importance?

Life insurance is a contract between you and an insurance company that provides a financial safety net for your loved ones in the event of your passing. It ensures your family's financial well-being is protected.

2. Types of Life Insurance Policies

There are various types of life insurance policies like term life, whole life, and universal life insurance, each serving different financial needs and goals.

3. How to Choose a Policy

Choosing the right life insurance policy depends on factors like age, income, health, and financial goals. Learn how to pick a policy that fits your needs.
